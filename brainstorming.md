## Evaluate and Categorize Top Ideas
1. **Tequila App**
    - **Description**: A party game that allows user to put names of all participating players. App will then give out questions, games, dares, and different dynamics. Has a drinking game option.
    - **Mobile**: For when on the move like roadtrips, parties, or want a quick fun activity. Users can participate in audio challenges, connect on the same session with others.
    - **Story**: Have a fun activity without the need to bring anything extra. Can become an icebreaker, friendship booster, an activity to turn a good time into a great time.
    - **Market**: 18+! Some challenges may include drinking and profanity. A great app for college students and young adults. With an option for multiple languages like English, Spanish, French, etc. The market can young people all around the world.
    - **Habit**: Users use this constantly on the weekends when going out with friends. It becomes a must to activity when going out, hanging out with friends or just enjoying a fun activity with company. 
    - **Scope**: Works very well with a simple version that just shuffles through challenges. Utilizing simple arrays and string placeholders. Including things like session joining, friend list, thematic challenges are not fundamental.

2. **Workout social media** 
    - **Description**: A social media for fitness. Allows users to tap in into friends workout sessions, mark personal records, share routines, photos and invite to workout together.
    - **Mobile**: Connect with friends at the gym. Snap a quick photo, share a real-time notification that you are working out, mark on a map if you worked out at a gym or in a park. 
    - **Story**: Make working out a fun, competitive, and bonding experience with friends een if far away. Create a healthy habit and push friends to be better, workout consistently and get in shape together. 
    - **Market**: People who workout, can be weightlifters, combat sports, yoga, running, calisthenics, endurance, sports. From ages 12-80. Anyone who works out can use this app. 
    - **Habit**: Users can interact with the app many times througout the day. Planning, sharing, using a workout. With features like notifications and real time alers, app can become habbit forming.
    - **Scope**: Can start very simple by sharing texts of workout descriptions, having personal records, adding friends. Progress into sharing images, workout locations, calendar invites. 
## FINAL DECISION:
###    Workout social media
